About The Event

About The Event

The word ‘melange’ itself means a mixture and true to its name the event was a conglomeration in every sense of the word.

Our vision is to engage the CSE Department as well as its global community to supoport and advance the program's excellence. Forster a life long spirit of belonging and pride by connecting alumni, students and faculty of CSE Department to each other.

Striving to be an industry leader in alumni relations through a creative and transformational business model. Build a unique industry based practive innocation team that positions the NSEC CSE Alumni Association at the forefront of technology. Persuit of excellence through agility and responsiveness to stakeholders and the environment. Develop a performance culture that attracts the best talent and makes the NSEC CSE Alumni Association a "center of excellence". Strengthen the communications portal between alumni and the CSE Department for lifelong alumni connectivity.

F.A.Q

  • When and where is Melange'19 going to be held?

    Melange'19 is gonna be held in Netaji Subhash Engineering College on the 28th of September, Saturday. It is scheduled to start from 10:00 AM and continue upto 7 in the evening.

  • How to reach the venue?

    Transport is available from the nearby New Garia Metro Station(Kavi Subhash) and also from Garia Metro station.

  • What is Melange'19 all about?

    The students of the CSE Department of NSEC have come together to organise this alumni meet. There shall be some stage performances and some unexpected surprises for the visitors as well. So hold your horses!

  • What is the theme for Melange'19?

    Melange'19 is based on the theme 'Back to Childhood'.

  • Is it compulsory to provide donation?

    It's no way mandatory to provide donation. However, any contribution to the success of the event is warmly entertained.

  • How much is the amount of donation?

    You are free to contribute at your own will. There is no fixed amount. We have kept it as minimal as ₹500.
